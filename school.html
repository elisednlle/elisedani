My Pre-School Experience

I finished my Pre-school Education at Headstart tutorial and learning house in Las-Pinas city. I dont remember much about this phase of my life but what I do know is I was always actively participating in school activities. I also sang a song during our completion ceremony. I made lots of friends and also brought my favorite stuffed toy with me even in school.

My Elementary Experince

I wemt to Antique SPED Center (now called Antique Integrated School) for my elementary education. I always strived to be in the top of my class. I joined multiple organizations during this phase of mine. I became the Vice President of the English Club, joined the Girl Scout and twinklers investure, and also playes volleyball for one semester. Some of my friends from elementary school are still my friends up until now. I was always so active and school activities. I really enjoyed my Elementary experience as it helped shape who I have become today.

My Highschool Experience

I am now currently in highschool studying at Antique National School in the Special Program For the Arts. I am not really active in school activities but I try my best to be a part in some of them. I learned lots of things including lessons that I can carry until I grow old. I made new friends; people that fills my day with laughter and whom I can lean on when I have problems. They have been my buddies during school events, study sessions, or just random hangouts during we have vacant time. I am always grateful for having them as my friends and for being a part of my highschool journey.
