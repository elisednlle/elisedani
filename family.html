My Family

I grew up in a nuclear family, with my parents and siblings in one household. My family values love, responsibility, and most importantly our relationship with God. We always make time for eachother, especially during important events for us to show that we care and value every success and achievement that we make. Our house is full of chaos but also filled with love and care. I am grateful to have them as my family.

My Mother

My mother is a Teacher and a Businesswoman. She is very hardworking and is always willing to lend a hand to those who are in need. She also loves and adores plants. She sacrifices her own needs just to provide us everything that we need. Despite being tired from work, she still makes sure that her family is well taken care of. Her love is unconditional. I will always be grateful for everything that she has done for me.

My Father

My father a responsible and hardworking man. He is also loving and patient but is strict when teaching me life lessons. He is the person who always encourages me to follow the words of God. He always makes sure that I have everything I need. He is not an affectionate person but he shows his love for us through his actions. I am very thankful to have him as my father.

My younger Brother

My younger brother is always full of energy. He is 2 years younger than me and is currently in 8th grade. He loves playing games and playing with our dogs. He is creative and always comes up with new ideas. He may be a little troublemaker at times, but he is the one who brings joy and laughter into our family.
